KEY MANAGEMENT PROFILE

ELVIS AHIOR

(Managing Partner)

An astute professional with over 15 years' experience in leading complex deals in both commercial & corporate banking as well as logistics, maritime, oil & gas, power, entertainment, technology and hospitality sectors. Elvis is quite vast in his experience in human resources, banking, entertainment and supply chain management and has brought this on board to drive the growth of several organizations. Elvis has led some the largest projects in the Live Event space including Olamide Live in Concert 1, 2 & 3, Davido's 30 Billion Concert, BurnaLive, FutureLive as well as the Edo Youth Mega Concert grossing revenues as high as N0.5billion for a single Concert. Elvis has equally handled complex privatization deals in the power sector worth US$754M

Sola Raji

Technical/Project Director

Sola started his journey in the entertainment industry as a musician but quickly fell in love with audio technology and started studying to become an studio engineer. Over the years he has grown and matured in the audio world to become a seasoned world class audio engineer. While growing in the audio industry, Sola has been exposed to other technologies and has taken advantage of this exposure to acquire more skills in lighting and events production. Sola has spent more time as a world class event manager in large format events and designed complex audio solutions for commercial spaces, houses of worship and corporate clients in the United States of America and Nigeria. Some of the notable projects include One Africa Music Festival across 4 continents, Flytime Festival etc..
